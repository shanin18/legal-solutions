The Legal Practice Area

A legal practice area is a specific field of law that a lawyer or law firm specializes in.
There are many more legal practice areas, each with its own set of laws.

Business Law

Business law is a broad field of law that deals with the legal issues and regulations related to businesses and commercial transactions.

Criminal Law

This area of law deals with crimes and their punishments. Lawyers in this field represent clients who have been charged with crimes.

Child Support

Child support is a payment made by a non-custodial parent to a custodial parent for the financial support of their child or children.

Education Law

Education law refers to the laws and regulations that govern the operation and administration of schools, as well as the responsibilities.

Divorce Law

Divorce law refers to the legal process and requirements for ending a marriage. The laws regarding divorce state, resolving issues.

Tax Law

Tax law refers to the laws and regulations that govern the assessment and collection of taxes. This includes federal, state, and local taxes.
